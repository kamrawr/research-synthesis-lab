Community Resilience and Rental Transformation Initiative

Empowering communities through collaborative rental market transformation

Program Overview

The Community Resilience and Rental Transformation Initiative empowers community-based organizations (CBOs) to enhance participation and engagement among tenants, landlords, and contractors in the rental market. This program addresses critical barriers to participation and resource access, fostering a collaborative environment that drives energy efficiency upgrades, tenant stability, and sustainable community development.
